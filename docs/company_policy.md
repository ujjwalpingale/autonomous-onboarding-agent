# Company Policy

**Working Hours**: Core working hours are 10 AM to 3 PM in your local timezone. Outside of those hours, you have flex time.
**Equipment**: The company provides a MacBook Pro, a monitor, and a $500 home office stipend.
**Vacation**: We have an unlimited PTO policy. We encourage all employees to take at least 3 weeks off per year.
**Security**: Remember to lock your screen when stepping away from your desk. Do not share API keys in public Slack channels.
